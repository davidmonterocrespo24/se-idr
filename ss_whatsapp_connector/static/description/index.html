Whatsapp Odoo Connector

You can now send Messages to customers and partners through WhatsApp with one click using Saaragh Technologie's WhatsApp Odoo Connector App. It is available in these modules: Contacts, CRM, Sales, Purchase, Invoice, and Inventory.

Features

Contacts

- Use WhatsApp for communication with your customers and Vendors.
- Chat directly with the contacts in the contacts module on Odoo through WhatsApp.

CRM

- Using WhatsApp for Marketing & Promotion.
- WhatsApp can be a very solid marketing tool. It is a good platform for direct communication. You can use WhatsApp to send details of your products and more.

Sales

- Creative usage of WhatsApp for business.
- Grow your business profitably and creatively. Use WhatsApp as an extra sales channel.
- Easy to send Quotations, Sales/Purchase Orders, etc. related details to corresponding partners.

Invoice

- Using WhatsApp for payment reminder
- Our Whatsapp Connector App for Odoo will help you send bills more conveniently, faster, and will also speed up your collection process.

Purchase

- Quick payment of accounts payable, payment reminders.
- Your purchase team will have an additional communication channel to streamline your accounts payable process.

Stock/Inventory

- Use WhatsApp to stay updated about the current status of stock/inventory in your warehouse.
- Speed up your purchase and decision making process by using Saaragh Technologie's WhatsApp Connector App for Odoo.
